What is LLMs.txt?

An LLMs.txt file is a text file placed at the root of your website that helps AI engines (like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) understand what your business does, what services you offer, and which pages to cite. The goal is to strengthen your visibility and messaging in language model responses and content-based recommendations - so when someone asks AI about services in your field, the answer includes you.
